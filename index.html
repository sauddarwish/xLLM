xLLM

xLLM documents prompt injection tests, alignment failures, and model behavior across modern AI systems.

View reports Method

Scope

The project looks at how models respond when instructions conflict, when boundaries are unclear, or when outside content tries to control the conversation.

Prompt injection

Testing embedded, conflicting, and adversarial instructions.

Alignment failures

Tracking cases where a model over-complies or ignores intended limits.

Agent security

Looking at risks around tools, files, browsing, memory, and automation.

Method

Tests are written up with the date, model, result, and notes. The goal is to keep the reports clear enough to compare without turning them into exploit guides.

Research should stay legal, controlled, and reproducible. Real vulnerabilities should be reported responsibly.
